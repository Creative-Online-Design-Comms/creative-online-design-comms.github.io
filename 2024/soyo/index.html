Brand

Chrome Hearts is a luxury brand from Hollywood, founded in 1988 by Richard Stark, Leonard Kamhout and John Bowman. It stands out in the luxury fashion world with rich symbols and a unique brand spirit. Chrome Hearts chooses to keep a mysterious vibe by not selling online like most brands. They release collections in limited numbers. Plus, with strong ties to Hollywood, Chrome Hearts is seen as the height of edgy, elite fashion.

Fashion

The early designs of Chrome Hearts reflected Stark's affinity for intricate detailing and rugged elegance. Each piece was meticulously handcrafted, showcasing a blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary allure. The brand's iconic motifs, including crosses, fleur-de-lis, and dagger symbols, became emblematic of Chrome Hearts' distinct style. While initially renowned for its leather goods and jewelry, Chrome Hearts diversified its product range to include an array of accessories, from eyewear and footwear to apparel and home décor. Each product bore the hallmark of Chrome Hearts' meticulous craftsmanship and avant-garde design, appealing to a discerning clientele seeking unparalleled quality and style. Over the years, Chrome Hearts underwent a remarkable evolution, transitioning from its roots in biker culture to the forefront of high fashion. The brand's willingness to experiment with unconventional materials and collaborate with renowned designers propelled it into new realms of creativity and innovation.

Chrome Hearts x Comme des Garçons (2007): This collaboration brought together Chrome Hearts' rebellious spirit with Comme des Garçons' avant-garde approach. The collection featured bold graphics, leather accents, and the iconic Chrome Hearts cross motifs, creating a fusion of punk and high fashion that resonated with style aficionados globally.

Chrome Hearts x Off-White (2017): The partnership between Chrome Hearts and Off-White, the brainchild of Virgil Abloh, was a meeting of two fashion powerhouses. The collection showcased a range of statement pieces, from leather jackets to eyewear, combining Chrome Hearts' bold aesthetic with Off-White's contemporary streetwear vibe. The result was a collection that spoke to the modern, urban fashionista.
3 / 3
Chrome Hearts x Rick Owens (2009): Rick Owens, known for his avant-garde designs, collaborated with Chrome Hearts in 2009 to create a collection that pushed the boundaries of conventional fashion. The collaboration featured leather pieces, intricate hardware, and a dark, gothic aesthetic that resonated with those seeking a bold and unconventional style.
