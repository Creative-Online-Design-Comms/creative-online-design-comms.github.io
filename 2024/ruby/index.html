About Me!

Iâ€™ve lived in Dunedin my whole life I love the way New Zealand changes depending on where you go. My favourite part about about Dunedin city is the way modern architecture combines with brutalism and Victorian in a way you can walk 20 meters and get a completely different feel, it makes it a perfect destination for photography, one of my favourite buildings to photograph is the Dunedin Railway Station. The colours of the aging copper along with the terracotta roof shingles paired with the light Oamaru stone with the dark basalt makes a perfect contrast. It is an incredible building to photograph from any angle. I started Photography in early June 2023, Some of the first things I captured were birds and cows. I take my camera with me on every holiday we go. I love animals and being able to Photograph them is one of the best things in the world.

Equipment I Use!

My camera is the canon eos 60d, I use two different lenses depending on what I'm photographing, My main lens is my Canon lens with 18-180mm, My lens i use for animal photography or close up shots I use my Tamron lens 18-270mm.
