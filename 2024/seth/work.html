My Music

Below are my two Music projects.
Under the album art are song previews, and clicking on the album art takes you to that project's website.

"Seth Fleming" Project

This is my project where I release solo music under my name. This project was born after wanting to put my guitar skills into a small EP. This album was also made dedicated to my boyfriend, as we've always said we have a 'Sun and Moon' relationship dynamic and all the song names being his online usernames over the time we have been dating.
Sun & Moon is a solo guitar album that is mainly improvised. I went for this approach because it feels like it came from my mind and heart at that very moment of recording, and there's a very low chance of writing the exact same thing again.

"Emperor Goblin" Project

This project is much more experimental than my previous work. Emperor Goblin was made after messing around in Garageband, my first experience working inside a DAW. After making "bad" music on purpose to call it "experimental", I created this sound I really enjoyed, and during my time making music for this project, I would include multigenre elements into my albums, including metal, breakcore and fusion.
Emperor Goblin was also an attempt at making a character & universe inside the project, Similar to Gorillaz and Ninja Sex Party.
