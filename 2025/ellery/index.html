UP-COMING COMPETITIONS

SOUTHERN GOLDIES

The Southern Goldies competition celebrates the passion and dedication of experienced players, bringing together athletes aged 40 and above for a weekend of skillful rallies and friendly rivalries. Scheduled for March 22nd, 2025 at the prestigious Harbour Tennis Complex, this event offers both singles and doubles matches across multiple categories.

VALLEY HITS

For younger players looking to test their abilities, the Valley Hits tournament is set for April 12th, 2025 at the lively Riverside Sports Arena. Open to competitors aged 16–30, this fast-paced event focuses on energy, skill, and determination. Featuring knockout singles and doubles draws, Valley Hits encourages participants to push their limits and rise to the challenge.

HOT SHOTS

The Hot Shots competition is a family-friendly event welcoming players of all ages 12 and up, making it the most inclusive tournament of the season. Taking place on May 3rd, 2025 at the scenic Greenfield Club Grounds, this event encourages both new and seasoned players to join in a day of tennis, fun, and community spirit.

ABOUT US

Welcome to our Tennis Club, a vibrant community where passion for the game brings players of all ages and skill levels together. Established to provide high-quality facilities and a welcoming environment, we offer everything from competitive tournaments to fun social events.

Our mission is to foster a love of tennis through accessible programs, expert coaching, and inclusive activities. From juniors to adults, casual players to competitors, we aim to create opportunities that inspire improvement, teamwork, and a lifelong appreciation for the sport.
