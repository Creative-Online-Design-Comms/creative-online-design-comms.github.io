OUR STORY

Atelier Crown

Founded in 1972 on the wild, windswept shores of New Zealand, Atelier Crown began as a humble atelier devoted to the art of handcrafted jewelery.

What started in a small workshop has grown into a regarded house of luxury.

Yet, our essence remains unchanged.

From Aotearoa to the world this is our story.
