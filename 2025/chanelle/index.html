Practical Tools to get you Started

Welcome to your easy access well-being enhancing tool box. Each tile contains a well-being tool aimed to manage stress and ground your body and mind in the present moment. Click on a tile to access step by step instructions.

Image by Rosy / Bad Homburg / Germany from Pixabay

Box Breathing

Box breathing is just one of many deep techniques that involves slowly breathing in and out to help you feel calmer and ground you in the present moment.

Illustration by Nhi NNhi from Pixabay

Mindful Watching

In this active mindfulness technique you can use in any environment. You will be guided to calmly notice colours in your surroundings.

Illustration by Hanin Abouzeid from Pixabay

Noticing Sensations

You will be guided to identify sensations in your body. This skills can be applied in stressful times or as a regular mindfulness practice.

Illustration by Hanin Abouzeid from Pixabay

Gratitude Practice

This exercise will guide on how to add more gratitude into your daily life. Gratitude is associated with enhanced well being and fosters resilience.

Illustration by Kat Sampang from Pixabay

Re-framing Thoughts

This tool can help to identify unhelpful thought patterns. Learning this skill can improve your well-being over time and decrease symptoms of anxiety.
