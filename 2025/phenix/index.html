Introduction to Dunedin

Dunedin is a historic and scenic city located on the southeast coast of the South Island. Often referred to as the “Edinburgh of the South,” it has strong Scottish roots and is known for it's well preserved Victorian and Edwardian architecture.

Dunedin is home to the University of Otago, the country's oldest university, which gives the city a lively student atmosphere. Surrounded by rugged coastlines and rolling hills, it offers unique wildlife experiences, including rare yellow-eyed penguins, royal albatrosses, and sea lions on the nearby Otago Peninsula.

The city also features attractions like Dunedin Railway station, Larnach castle, Baldwin Street - once recognized as the steepest street in the world. Dunedin combines natural beauty, cultural richness, and a relaxed lifestyle making it a unique destination in New Zealand.

Graze and Glow

Open Mon - Fri 5:00pm - 10:00pm

About
Graze and Glow is located in lower Stewart Street just down from the Octagon. This is a modern restaurant where everything on the menu is $9. This is a indulgent small place lit up with neon lit interior. To order just simply scan the QR code at your table and order so make sure your phone isn't on red. The menu is top to toe small bites from pretty much every cuisine on the planet, from tacos and croquettes to lamb skewers and gnocchi. All are shareable but you can definitely demolish the plates without anyone’s help. Service is fast so be prepared for an overflow of cocktails and dishes.

The Bog Irish Bar

Open Mon - Wed 4:00pm - 10:00pm
Thurs - 4:00pm - 11:00pm
Fri - Sun 4:00pm - midnight

About
Where every day is your lucky day! Unwind with a touch of Irish charm at The Bog’s Irish Bar. Social Hour running every Monday to Friday from 4–6pm. Enjoy $9 house wines plus $10.50 tap beers and spirits. Come early, stay late. The Bog also has to offer, feeds that are full of flavor and Irish charm. The bog serves up hearty favorites and bold flavors that hit the spot every time. Thick rich slow cooked stews, juicy burgers, and classic pub plates that never disappoint. While enjoying your Beer or feed you also get a live preforming band that comes with it for free.

Joe's Garage

Open Mon - Sun 7:30am - 10:00pm

About
Joe's Garage is a casual, laid-back restaurant known for its hearty comfort food, friendly atmosphere, and quirky garage-themed décor. Located in a bustling part of town, it draws locals and visitors alike with it's diverse menu featuring juicy burgers, loaded fries, sizzling steaks, and classic milkshakes. The interior is filled with vintage car memorabilia, giving it a nostalgic charm that sets it apart from typical diners. Joe's Garage offers a warm, welcoming environment and consistently satisfying food that keeps customers coming back.

Catacombs Night Club

Open Fri - Sat 10:00pm - 3:00am

About
Catacombs is a night club located in the Octagon in Dunedin. Regularly open Friday and Saturday nights, typically running from 10pm - 3am. Catacombs Nightclub is a standout venue in Dunedin, offering more than just music—it's a theatrical, genre-spanning nightclub with a twist of VIP exclusivity. Whether you're drawn to booming beats, themed throwback nights, or just soaking in a vibrant, atmospheric space, Catacombs delivers a memorable night out.
