Welcome!


Hi, I’m Emily, a self-taught jewellery artist specializing in wire-wrapping and seed beading. Inspired by nature and a lifelong love of creativity, each piece is crafted with care to bring joy, beauty, and a personal touch to your everyday style. Thanks for supporting my hand-crafted work!

I'm Emily Wister, the creator of Wisteria Whimsy and the sole designer behind each hand-crafted piece. I began making jewellery seriously after moving to New Zealand in 2020. Entirely self-taught, I’ve spent the past six years refining my craft through wire-wrapping and seed beading. Jewellery, to me, is more than an accessory it's a way to share beauty, creativity, and joy. Every piece is made with care and intention, and I hope you find something here that speaks to you.
