About Me



Hi! I’m Emily Wister, the creator of Wisteria Whimsy and the sole designer behind every piece of jewellery you see here. Thanks to a family that loved nature and encouraged artistic expression I was raised in an environment that fostered my skills that I use to this day. That early connection to beauty and creativity has stayed with me ever since. I moved to New Zealand in 2020, and it was here that I began taking jewellery-making seriously. I’ve been creating handmade jewellery for about six years now being entirely self-taught through lots of trial and error and a lot of passion.

While I’ve come a long way, I’m still learning every day, evolving my artistic expression and that’s part of what I love most about this craft. My specialties are wire-wrapping and seed beading, and I’m drawn to the intricate, delicate nature of hand-crafted design. For me, jewellery is more than just accessories but a way to express my creativity, love of all things beautiful, and share happiness with others. I create each piece with care, not just for the love of the art, but because I know how much happiness a hand-crafted piece can bring to someone else. That connection is what inspires me most.
Thank you for being here and supporting my handmade jewellery. I hope you find something that speaks to you.
