Basic Elements

Zaha Hadid was an Iraqi born British Architect. She was the first woman to have ever claimed the Pritzker Prize, The Pritzker Prize is the most valued award across architecture. Zaha Hadid was mostly known for her futuristic designs spread across the world. The building I replicated in a sketch was the Heydar Aliyev Centre. The Heydar Aliyev Centre was designed in 2012 by Zaha Hadid and the structure is located in Baku, Azerbaijan. Zaha Hadid passed away in 2016 due to a sudden heart attack while being treated for her bronchitis. Zaha Hadid's name still lives strong and her firm she created in 1979 is still completing projects to this day.

Frank Lloyd Wright was an American architect that Had over 1000 different architectural design, over 500 of which were actually recognised. Frank Lloyed Wright is well recognised and has designed many stunning structures in his life. I incorporated two pieces that he has done before as they show how vastly different his designs were for each client. Frank has shaped architecture in so many ways and his work is a staple of architectural history.

Ludwig mies van der Rohe was a German-American architect known for his minimalist and functionalist designs. One of his most iconics pieces is the Farnsworth House, a sleek modern retreat built in 1951. Located in the Illinois, the house is raised above the floodplain, blending seamlessly into the surrounding landscape. With its simple, rectangular design and its extensive use of glass, the Farnsworth House is a perfect example of Ludwig's saying "less is more" The structures clean lines and open spaces create a sense of freedom and connection to nature, making this building a masterpiece.

Fariborz is an Indian and Iranian architect known for his stunning designs that blend spiritually and modernity. One of his most iconic works is the Lotus Temple located in New Delhi, India. This breathtaking structure is shaped like a lotus flower, with 27 marble "petals" that seem to float on water.The temple's design is not only beautiful but also symbolic, representing the unity and diversity of humanity. With its serene atmosphere and stunning architecture, the Lotus temple is the most visited building in the world.

Peter Aldington, John Craig, and Paul Collinge are incredible british architects who designed the famous Clayton House in London. Finished in 1992, this stunning residence showcases their innovative approach to modern architecture. The home features a striking curved glass Exterior and an incredibly unique roofline. The Clayton House is a masterpiece of contemporary design, created by these three architects. Their use of bold shapes and materials creates a unique living space thats both functional and visually appealing.
