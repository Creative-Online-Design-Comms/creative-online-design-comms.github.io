About Me

My name is Amelia Cook and I am striving to become a young architect. I created this website to hold pieces that I have created of famous buildings. These are some buildings I have come across in research I have used in previous assessments. These pieces show my dedication and love for architecture in so many ways and this website has allowed me to learn more skills which I will be able to take into my future career.

About Website

This website is created in the style of a portfolio to hold the work I have created based on famous buildings across the world. I have never created a website before so I hope the information you may be looking for is easy to find. This website contains different pages some containing information of the architects behind the sketches I have replicated and other pages contain my work and information on where to find me.
