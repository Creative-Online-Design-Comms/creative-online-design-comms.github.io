About Me

Hello, my name is ANQU, also known as Adrienne. I’m a student who enjoys creating digital content, whether it’s editing videos, drawing, or animating

My art style blends influences from various media, I often lean towards either anime or semi-realism. I tend to experiment with light and shadows to help shape mood and depth. Lately, I’ve been exploring dynamic poses and movement to bring more energy into my work.

I have experience with Clip Studio Paint, Ibis Paint, Photoshop, Alight Motion, and Premiere Pro. While I’m still a beginner with video editing software, I am quite comfortable using drawing and image editing programs.
